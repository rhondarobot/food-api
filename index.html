Welcome!

Find your next recipe based on YOUR preferences and find out how long you would need to walk to burn off a serving. Type in your weight and search. Then, drag your mouse over your recipe results to get calories per serving and walking instructions.

Want to search for gluten-free recipes? Type in 'gluten-free' in the search bar. Want to search by vitamins or minerals? Try typing in 'vitamin C' or 'calcium', for example. Tracking calorie intake? Search for recipes that fall within your meal limits.

Tap on the recipe name for more info about calories per serving and how many minutes you need to walk to burn off that yummy food!
